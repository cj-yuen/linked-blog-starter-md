$$A \cup B$$
>set containing elements that are either in $A$ or $B$ 
>	ie. $A =$ {Ron, Bob, Kelly} and $B =$ {Tim, Ryan, Bob} then $A\cup B=$ {Ron, Bob, Kelly, Tim, Ryan}
