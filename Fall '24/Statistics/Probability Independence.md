events $A$ and $B$ are <u>independent</u> if 
$$P(A\cap B) = P(A) \cdot P(B)$$
also if $P(A)>0$ and $P(B) > 0$, $\rightarrow$ then the following are the same:
- $A$ and $B$ are <u>independent</u> 
- $P(A|B) = P(A)$ 
- $P(B|A) = P(B)$
